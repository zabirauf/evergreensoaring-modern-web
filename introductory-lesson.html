Introductory Lesson

Experience the thrill of soaring flight with an introductory lesson at Evergreen Soaring. Our FAA Certificated Glider Flight Instructors will guide you through your first glider flight experience, teaching you the basics of soaring flight and aircraft control.

Lesson Details

  • At least 30 minutes of ground instruction
  • One flight to 3,000 feet above ground
  • Hands-on experience with aircraft controls
  • Glider Log Book to start your pilot career
  • $200 plus tax (includes all of the above)

Special Offer:

$50 of your intro lesson fee will be credited towards the club initiation fee if you apply for membership within 30 days.

Frequently Asked Questions

What forms of payment are accepted?

We accept cash or check for the $200 plus tax fee.

Are there any limitations?

  • Maximum weight: 240 lbs
  • Maximum height: approximately 6 feet 4 inches
  • Age: Typically 10 years and up (must be able to follow instructions and see out the window)
  • Mobility: Should be able to enter/exit a deep, narrow space

How do I schedule a lesson?

Use the contact form below. A club member will reach out to discuss logistics.

Where are the flights conducted?

Meet at the Arlington Municipal Airport office (18204 59th Dr NE, Arlington). You'll be escorted to the glider operations area.

View Arlington Municipal Airport Map

What should I bring?

  • Hat (no caps with buttons on top)
  • Sunscreen
  • Sunglasses
  • Water
  • Camera or smartphone (optional)
  • Payment (cash or check)
