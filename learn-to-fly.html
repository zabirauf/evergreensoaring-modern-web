Learn to Fly at Evergreen Soaring

Free Flight Instruction for Members

Evergreen Soaring provides free flight instruction for members. Glider pilots are licensed by the FAA, just like powered aircraft pilots. You'll learn to:

  • Control the glider safely and precisely
  • Navigate effectively
  • Develop a basic understanding of weather and other aviation topics

Getting Started
